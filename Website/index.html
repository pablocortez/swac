Values

Today, coaches and tutors in a variety of industries either give out free advice and coaching, or try to charge for it in blocks that most people either do not want to pay, or simply do not pay for due to inconvenience. To be a successful coach, a would-be Mentor needs to be able to monetize time and knowledge, which involves a seamless solution that makes payment and collections not only simple and painless, but also a pleasure to use with our platform.

The Mentor plan offers a wide range of opportunities available to people all over the world. To begin with, Mentor allows the individual to gain knowledge and expertise in a field that was not previously accessible to him/her. For example, an individual who is interested in learning how to play soccer but does not have access to a trainer/coach in his area can log onto Mentor and find the right expert for him. Furthermore, Mentor offers the opportunity for experts to share their expertise in a particular field and be able to make a profit. Often times, there are many individuals who get discouraged from sharing their knowledge with the world but with the monetary incentive, these individuals can have the ability to empower the world through their expertise. Lastly, Mentor allows the public to stay in touch with real-life experts on-the-go and even 24/7. Many people experience struggles and problems that can take days or even weeks to resolve by going to an expert in their area. With Mentor, we give these people the opportunity to avoid the stress and resolve their problem on the spot.

Then

The most difficult aspect of our plan will be introducing it to the public and making it well known. In the beginning, you will need an invite code to become a mentor and once you become a mentor you will receive five invite codes that you will be able to give out to invite people to become mentors as well. The initial launch of our business plan will grow at a slower pace in order to deliver great experiences to all users and Mentors while the app goes through its early stages and development bugs are found and fixed. Mentor will gradually increase in demand as our name gains popularity. Once our business becomes prominent, we will be able to manage our success and allow it to grow even farther through the use of customer feedback and effective marketing. Overall, the initial launch of Mentor will be feasible to our mentees but difficult for our mentors. As the business progresses, feasibility will become much more manageable.

Now

Once we have established a local customer base, we will begin to promote our business internationally and improve our strategies to conduct business internationally. The first thing we will do is perform a market study to understand the market's personality, economic feasibility, market trends, financial cost patterns and market forecasts. This will help us determine how to approach this new development and will help us recognize the changes that need to be made. We will create a translator that will be able to eliminate any language barriers that may occur. Another thing that will need to be added is a currency convertor which will have all of the foreign exchange rates. This will further the convenience of the platform by giving the customer all they need in one convenient location. To be successful in other countries, we will need to take into consideration and plan for all legal and regulatory barriers that may occur.

Overall, this seems like a great opportunity to expand our business and bridge different cultures.

Future

Las Vegas has poised itself as a hub of technological advancement and innovation. With large companies like Tesla and Switch investing millions of dollars in Nevada, the large industries like gambling and entertainment that currently dominate and propel Nevada’s economy are likely to merge with technology. It is not far fetched to see Nevada being the home of several tech startups, competing, but most importantly, collaborating, with Silicon Valley, its neighbor. The future of Nevada is being shaped by influential people like Governor Sandoval, Sen. Catherine Cortez-Masto, Zappos’ Tony Hsieh, Switch’s Rob Roy, and the hundreds of small business owners that are changing the landscape of Nevada’s economy. Our universities are becoming top-tier research institutions due to the hard work and collaboration of our community and the diversity of the student bodies. With different backgrounds and personal histories, Nevadans are creating a better future for not only the 2.8 million people that live here, but also for the hundreds of thousands of tourists that visit our state every year and enjoy what our Silver State has to offer.
